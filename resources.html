About The E-Cycle Hub

The E-Cycle Hub is an initiative dedicated to promoting responsible E-waste management through education and data-driven decision making. We believe that informed users are the first line of defense against the growing global e-waste crisis.

We are a project led by environmental advocates and IT professionals aiming to bridge the gap between technology consumption and sustainability practices.

**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):**

  • **Q: How is the 'Estimated Lifespan' calculated?** A: It is based on a simplified model considering the average functional age of components (CPU/RAM) and the initial purchase date.
  • **Q: Are your Recyclers certified?** A: We prioritize linking to nationally recognized, certified recycling programs (like R2 or e-Stewards certified facilities).
